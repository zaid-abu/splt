# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

adaptive

## Users

People splitting shared costs with friends and small groups in everyday social contexts like trips, meals, rent-adjacent household expenses, and informal paybacks. They need to capture expenses quickly, understand balances at a glance, and avoid awkward follow-up.

## Product Purpose

Splt helps people split money between friends and groups without friction. The product exists to make recording expenses, assigning shares, tracking who owes whom, and settling up feel fast, clear, and emotionally lightweight.

## Brand Personality

Warm, calm, trustworthy. The interface should feel premium but familiar, reducing tension around money rather than dramatizing it.

## Anti-references

Not flashy fintech with aggressive growth-product cues. Not a gamified social app competing for attention. Not cold accounting software that feels dense, corporate, or spreadsheet-led.

## Design Principles

- Make shared money feel simple, not stressful.
- Keep the next action obvious at every step.
- Favor familiar native mobile patterns over novelty.
- Use warmth to reduce tension, not to add clutter.
- Show balances and consequences clearly before confirmation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target solid readable contrast, clear non-color state cues, and screen-reader-friendly labels throughout. Respect reduced-motion preferences, maintain large touch targets that satisfy both iOS and Android guidance, and preserve safe-area and system navigation expectations on each platform.
